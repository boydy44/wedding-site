Disney Area

Walt Disney World is less than 10 minutes drive from the venue, any Walt Disney World Resort will be a reasonable distance for travel on the day of the wedding.

Universal Area

The Universal Orlando Resort is a little further out but is still only a 30 minute drive from the Venue. The venue will be accessible from any Universal resort hotel, it will just be a slightly longer commute.
