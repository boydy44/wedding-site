FAQ

Is the venue near the theme parks?

Walt Disney World resort is a 10 minute drive and Universal Orlando Resort is a 30 minute drive. You can drive or Uber to the theme parks however if you are staying in a Disney or Universal resort hotel most will provide transportation to the parks.

Will I need a car?

If you are planning to stay for a vacation it is easier to get around with a car however Uber is very popular and it is never difficult to get a ride.

Is the venue near a beach?

The venue is in Kissimmee which is pretty central however if you have a car there are some great beaches within driving distance.

Clearwater Beach - approx 1.5 hour drive

Melbourne Beach - approx 1.5 hour drive

Cocoa Beach - approx 1 hour drive
